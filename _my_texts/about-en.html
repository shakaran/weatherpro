About This Station

The station is powered by a weather station. The data is collected every 2 seconds and the site is updated every 1-5 minutes. This site and its data is collected using .

The station is comprised of an anemometer, a rain gauge, and a thermo-hygro sensor situated in optimal positions for highest accuracy possible.
